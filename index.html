About Us

The Fashion & Music Club is a space for students who love creativity, expression, and community. Whether you're into runway styling, streetwear design, DJing, producing beats, or discovering new artists, this club brings together the best of both worlds.

We host creative workshops, photoshoots, styling sessions, listening parties, collaboration meet-ups, and guest speakers from the fashion and music industries.
